Avoid the Bugs

You need to be fast and accurate in order to protect your precious code from bugs. On the way you will receive the help of your beloved friend rubber duck!

Instructions

  1. Press "Enter" to start the game.
  2. Press "Spacebar", or arrow up to jump.
  3. Press "Arrow down" to dug the bugs.
  4. Click anywhere inside the box or press "Spacebar" to restart the game.
